About Monerica

Monerica.com is an informational directory that documents businesses, services, and tools that accept Monero (XMR) and other cryptocurrencies. The project focuses on privacy-respecting commerce and censorship-resistant payments.

Monerica does not process payments, provide financial services, custody funds, or act as an intermediary. All information is collected from publicly available sources and community research.

Listings & Statuses

Listings on Monerica are categorized to help users assess risk and reliability. These statuses are informational only and do not constitute endorsements.

  • Admitted — Added to the directory but not tested by the community
  • Verified — Tested by at least one member of the community
  • Questionable — Uncertainty around claims or service reliability, but no confirmed reports of theft
  • Scam — Credible evidence that the service has taken user funds without delivering the promised product or service

Listing statuses may change over time as new information becomes available.

No Accounts

Monerica does not require user accounts and does not collect personal identifying information to browse the directory.
