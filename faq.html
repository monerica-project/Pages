How do Monerica sponsorships work?

Monerica offers three sponsorship types: Main Sponsor, Category Sponsor, and Subcategory Sponsor. Sponsorship increases visibility across Monerica (homepage placement + directory visibility), and includes distribution via RSS + weekly newsletter.

Sponsorship is first-come, first-serve. Current sponsors can renew before expiry to keep their placement. See details and rules on the Sponsored Listings page.

Requirements: Your listing must be in the directory for at least 90 days, and must be Admitted or Verified (cannot be Questionable or Scam). If a sponsored listing is later identified as a scam, or stops accepting Monero, it may be suspended without refund. (These requirements are listed on the Sponsored Listings page.)

What sponsor slots are available right now?

Availability changes frequently. The live, up-to-date availability (including waitlists and “notify me” links) is always shown here: Sponsored Listings.

Example (Exchanges): If you’re looking for a Main Sponsor or Category placement under Exchanges, check the live page above. As of the current listing status shown on that page:

  • Main Sponsor: can be full (max 5 at a time) and may show a “next availability” timestamp with a waitlist option.
  • Category Sponsor (Exchanges): can be unavailable while an active sponsor runs, and the page will display the expiration date/time and a notify link.
  • Subcategory Sponsor (Exchanges → Instant Swaps): can also be unavailable and will show its own expiration and waitlist option.

If a slot is unavailable, use the “Notify me of openings” / waitlist link shown next to that slot on the Sponsored Listings page.

What does sponsorship cost?

Current market rates (USD + XMR equivalents) are always listed here: Sponsored Listing Offers. Rates can change based on demand, and some subcategories have overrides.

Typical durations: 30, 60, 90, or 180 days.

Important note: The Offers page also shows whether a specific offer is “Buy” (available) or “Join Waitlist” (currently taken).

How do we start a sponsored listing (next steps)?

  1. Make sure your listing exists in the Monerica directory. Not listed yet? Submit it here: submission page.
  2. Confirm eligibility: listing must be 90+ days old and Admitted or Verified.
  3. Check availability (and join waitlists if needed): Sponsored Listings.
  4. Review pricing for your desired placement and duration: Sponsored Listing Offers.
  5. Buy sponsorship: choose type (Main / Category / Subcategory), pick your directory listing, select duration, and pay with Monero (XMR). Your sponsored listing activates automatically once payment confirms.

If you’re an exchange asking about “Main” or “Category under Exchanges” (example inquiry): check the Sponsored Listings page for the Exchanges category status and use the notify/waitlist link if it’s taken. If Main Sponsor is full, you can still sponsor a different category or a subcategory where available.

How do I renew or extend my sponsored listing?

Visit the active sponsored listings page and extend your listing without losing placement.

Are sponsorships endorsements?

No. Sponsored listings are paid placements and not endorsements.

What is Monerica?

Monerica is a Monero (XMR)–only directory focused on building a real Monero circular economy.

Every listing either directly accepts Monero or meaningfully supports its use.

How do I get verified / a green checkmark?

A green checkmark means the service has been confirmed to accept Monero in a real transaction.

  • Verified directly by Monerica, or
  • Proven through a public transaction report

Submit proof via find existing listing.

How do I submit a new link?

Use the submission page to add a Monero-accepting business or service.

How do I edit or update an existing listing?

Find your listing at find existing listing and submit updates for review.

How do I leave a review?

Reviews use PGP authentication via Kleopatra to prevent spam while preserving privacy. You enter your public key and confirm a message with it to grant permission.

Download: Kleopatra then: File > New Open PGP Key Pair

How do I report a scam?

Contact the business first. If unresolved, submit a detailed review with evidence.

Why are you using CloudFlare?

CloudFlare protects Monerica from repeated DDoS attacks. Use Tor or a VPN for privacy.
