Monerica FAQ

What is Monerica?

Monerica is a Monero only directory. It's a directory for a Monero circular economy. Every listing relates to, or directly accepts, the cryptocurrency Monero.

How do I get verified / a green checkmark?

A green checkmark means your business or service has been successfully tested using Monero (XMR). Verification requires a completed, paid transaction using Monero either:

  • Conducted directly by Monerica, or
  • Proven via a public post (e.g. forum, blog, video, or review site) by someone who completed a transaction.
If you have proof, please submit the link here as a note to the reviewer for your listing.

How do I leave a review?

Download Kleopatra to set up your PGP key. Once you have your public key, that is what you submit for a review. It will give you a text that only you can decrypt. Paste that in the notepad in the program and decrypt it. Then you can authenticate to leave a review.

If a site is conducting illegal activity, please submit a revision or contact us so that we can remove it.

Why are you using CloudFlare?

Monerica has experienced several DDoS attacks which nearly ended the project. The only practical and affordable way to defend against these as a small website is by using CloudFlare. You should use a VPN or access our Tor link for maximum privacy.
