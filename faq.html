What is Monerica?

Monerica is a Monero (XMR) only directory. It's a directory for a Monero circular economy. Every listing relates to, or directly accepts, the cryptocurrency Monero.

How do I get verified / a green checkmark?

A green checkmark means your business or service has been successfully tested using Monero (XMR).

  • Conducted directly by Monerica, or
  • Proven via a public post (forum, blog, video, or review site).

If you have proof, please submit the link here as a note to the reviewer.

How do I leave a review?

Download Kleopatra and create a PGP key (File → New OpenPGP Key Pair). Submit your public key to receive encrypted verification text you can decrypt to authenticate your review.

How do I report a scam?

Contact the company through all official channels first. If unresolved, submit a detailed review with supporting evidence.

Monerica enforces a zero-tolerance scam policy and may warn, remove listings, or notify the community when credible reports are confirmed.

Are sponsorships endorsements?

No. Monerica does not endorse or assume liability for sponsors’ actions, statements, or conduct.

How do I report an inactive site?

Find the listing at find existing listing, edit it, and submit for review.

What if a site is conducting illegal activity?

Please submit a revision or contact us so the listing can be reviewed and removed if necessary.

Why are you using CloudFlare?

Due to repeated DDoS attacks, CloudFlare is currently the only viable defense. For maximum privacy, use a VPN or access Monerica via Tor.

Are you using affiliate links?

Yes. Some links are affiliate links and may earn Monerica a commission.
