Listing Details

There are two types of advertising, Main Sponsor and Subcategory Sponsor.

See current offers and pricing: here.

Steps for listing:

  1. Select your listing (Note: your choice must currently be listed)
  2. Choose your listing's duration
  3. Pay for your sponsored listing
  4. Your listing goes live automatically

Main Sponsor

  • Sponsored listings are a way to get your selected listing seen by more people.
  • Sponsored listings are not recommendations to users, they are promotions paid to keep Monerica active.
  • A maximum of 5 sponsored listings are shown at the top of the directory.
  • The topmost listing is the one that has the longest duration of time left for its payment.
  • In addition to being at the top of the page, your listing is also highlighted in the normal list.
  • If your default link is an affiliate link, your non-affiliate link will be displayed when your sponsored listing is active
  • Sponsored listings are expected to have reliable service. If your listing is identified as a scam while you’re advertising, your sponsored listing will be suspended and you will not receive a refund!

  • If your default link is an affiliate link, your non-affiliate link will be displayed when your sponsored listing is active
  • Sponsored listings are expected to have reliable service. If your listing is identified as a scam while you’re advertising, your sponsored listing will be suspended and you will not receive a refund!
