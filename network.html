Monerica is the hub of a small family of independent, privacy-respecting projects for the Monero community. Each site references the others, so you can always find your way around the network.

Sites in the network

  • Monerica.com — you are here: the Monero circular-economy directory of merchants and services that accept XMR.
  • MoneroAcademy.com — free Monero education: courses, lessons and quizzes from beginner to advanced.
  • MoneroPriceNow.com — the live Monero (XMR) price, clean and tracker-free.
  • MoneroMarketCap.com — Monero market data, supply and statistics.
  • MoneroShameList.com — a watchlist of privacy-focused companies that don't yet accept Monero.
